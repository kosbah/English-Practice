The 75k Word Challenge & Shadow Operator System

Objective: Speak 75,000 words to remove the mental block, achieve fluency, and secure clients.
Current Status: Vocabulary is solid (pre-intermediate to intermediate), but sentence structure resembles a stream of consciousness.
Mindset: Authority comes from short, clear sentences. Long, winding sentences project uncertainty. By fixing the "and/so" habit, your thinking will sound more structured, logical, and senior.

1. Core Speaking Drills & Action Plan

  • 1. The Two-Second Rule: Break the "and/so/which is" train. Say one sentence. Pause in silence for two seconds. Start the next sentence.
  • 2. The Tense Anchor: Before speaking, pick one time frame (Past, Present, or Future) and stick to it. (e.g., Use "I decided, I went, I saw" — do not switch to "I go" halfway through).
  • 3. The Question Flip: English questions require an auxiliary verb before the subject, not Arabic statement order. (e.g., Do you think? How can I?). Check questions for do, does, is, or are.
  • 4. Input Correction (The Loop): When reviewing transcripts (Wispr Flow/ChatGPT), use Ctrl+F to actively hunt and delete specific recurring mistakes like "search about." Focus only on reviewing corrected mistakes so you understand where you went wrong, rather than rereading everything.
  • 5. The "Which Is" Ban: Delete "which is." Replace it with a direct word, a simple "is," or a colon.
  • 6. The Mirroring Technique: Generate 10 distinct questions for each of the five identified topics to practice speaking naturally without mental fatigue.
  • 7. Nuance Injection: Replace robotic phrases with natural ones. (e.g., Change “You understand me” to “You got me right.”)
  • 8. Word Count Tracking: Actively track your daily spoken word count as you work toward the 75k goal.

2. Shadow Operating Strategy & Risk Assessment

  • Target Channels: Educational niche, 1k - 50k subscribers (initially 5k-50k), posting 4+ videos/month, achieving 3x average view spikes.
  • Aesthetic: Prioritize raw value. Eliminate channels with overly polished/professional thumbnails.
  • Risk Assessment & Validation: You are balancing the satisfaction of learning to filter opportunities against the fear that this model is misleading. This fear mirrors previous concerns about committing four months to the AI system timeline.
  • The Fix: Set a firm 1-month deadline to evaluate the ROI of the Shadow Operating strategy. This provides a clear decision point and eliminates the fear of wasting time.

5. Vocabulary & Grammar Library

Core Verbs: Make vs. Do vs. Cause

  • Do: Do research, conduct a search.
  • Make: Make a plan, make a schedule, make a mistake, make progress.
  • Cause/Feel: Cause fatigue (or "make me tired"). Never say "make fatigue."

Sentence Structure & Phrasing Corrections

Incorrect / Direct Translation Native Correction / Rule
"I recorded a lot of content and I know how to fix this which is I use ChatGPT so I have the material." "I recorded a lot of content. I know how to fix this by using ChatGPT. Now I have the material."
"My first goal which is plug removal." "My first goal is plug removal."
"I know how to fix this, which is I use ChatGPT." "I know how to fix this: I use ChatGPT."
Mixing connectors like "so but" Split into two sentences and use "However".
"Will affect me good or not?" "Will it affect me well?"
"How many words a day I have to speak?" "How many words a day do I have to speak?"
"Do you think which tool I have to use it?" "Which tool do you think I should use?" (Drop the 'it' at the end).

Prepositions & Collocations (Stop Translating)

  • Search for (Not search about)
  • Compare A and B (Not compare between A and B)
  • Look to see if they're active (Not look about if they're active)
  • Look for people (Not look to people)
  • Based solely on my knowledge (Not based on my knowledge only)
  • Create a schedule / Set a schedule (Not make a schedule)
  • Turn them into packages (Not make them as packages)
  • I didn't use to speak (Not I usually use to not speak)
  • I feel deeply that (Not I feel deep that)
  • Deep down / Deep in my heart (Not the deep inside my heart)
  • I have achieved my first goal, which is... (Not I achieve my first goal which is)

6. AI Prompt Optimization Template

When using ChatGPT or Gemini to process spoken notes, strict adherence is non-negotiable. The AI must not improvise or add its own ideas. Use this exact prompt:

"I am going to provide a transcript of my spoken notes. These notes include business strategy related to Shadow Operating and YouTube, personal reflection, and action planning.

Your role is to act as my executive assistant and editor. Convert this raw text into a structured, professional document.

Requirements:

  • Strict adherence: Do not hallucinate, improvise, or add ideas that did not come from me.
  • Create a clear and relevant title.
  • Break the content into logical sections with a headline and a subheadline.
  • Rewrite my spoken words into clean, professional English while preserving my tone and terminology.
  • Remove repetition and fix grammar.
  • Extract an action plan as a checklist.

Here is the transcript: [paste text here]"
