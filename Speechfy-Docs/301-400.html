English Training Vocabulary

🔹 3️⃣0️⃣1️⃣ Edgy

Meaning: Trying to disrupt the market by acting rebellious, bold, or contrarian just for the clout and attention.
Example: “The new ad copy tries way too hard to sound edgy and just looks like an amateur trying to go viral.”

🔹 3️⃣0️⃣2️⃣ Groundbreaking

Meaning: A massive paradigm shift; a 10x innovation that completely disrupts the industry and forces competitors to adapt or die.
Example: “That new AI backend integration wasn't just an update, it was completely groundbreaking for our margins.”

🔹 3️⃣0️⃣3️⃣ Edgy and Groundbreaking

Meaning: The holy grail of branding—disrupting the market while actually delivering a massive, undeniable 10x ROI.
Example: “The founder pitched a SaaS that was both edgy and groundbreaking, instantly securing VC funding and dominating the niche.”

🔹 3️⃣0️⃣4️⃣ Intermittent Hypoxia

Meaning: The hardcore biohack of starving your brain of oxygen in bursts to force biological adaptation and build mental resilience.
Example: “He does intense breathwork to induce intermittent hypoxia before jumping into a $100k negotiation to lock in his focus.”

🔹 3️⃣0️⃣5️⃣ Myelin Sheath

Meaning: The biological wiring around your nerves that you upgrade through brutal repetition, making your execution 10x faster.
Example: “Doing 10,000 cold calls builds the myelin sheath so you handle aggressive objections automatically without blinking.”

🔹 3️⃣0️⃣6️⃣ Big Medicine / Big Pharma
