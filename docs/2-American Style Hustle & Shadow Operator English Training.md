# American Style Hustle & Shadow Operator English Training

## Phase 1: The Mindset (The Miami / Modern American English Rewrite)

**The Old Way:**
"Okay, hello my friend, good morning. I need your help. I expected you to tell me a step-by-step, small action plan to pull a script from a video on my PC..."

**The Modern American/Miami Hustle Way:**
"Hey man, good morning. I need a quick favor. I was hoping you could give me a short, step-by-step game plan on how to pull a transcript from a video on my PC. The video is huge—like 500 MB—so I'm thinking about converting it to MP3 or some other audio format first just to shrink the file size, since I don't even need the visuals. Once that's done, we can figure out how to grab the text. Let me know what you think the best move is."

## Phase 2: Pronunciation & Flow Training

**Rules applied:**
* CAPITAL letters = stress
* [brackets] = simplified pronunciation
* commas / slashes = speaking rhythm
* reductions = natural spoken English (wanna, gonna, fer em)
